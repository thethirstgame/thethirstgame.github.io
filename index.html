▶ GAME INFO

HUD
You normally see only Water, Food and Stamina.

To see detailed HUD:
• Unarmed → crouch and wait ~20 seconds
• Armed (pistol/rifle) → open inventory (G)

Interaction
• Go unarmed and crouch to interact
• Actions take time — you must commit

Action Speed
• Fastest when unarmed
• Slower with pistol
• Slowest with rifle lowered (double tap CTRL)

World & Items
• Carry water and food in containers
• Vehicles can be refueled and repaired
• Wheels can be fixed in the field

Dropping Items
• Unarmed + crouched
• Hold ~20 seconds

Saving
• Sit (default ') for ~180 seconds
• Use action menu → Save

Important:
• Items do NOT persist on logout
• Stash them in the world or lose them
