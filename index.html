▶ GAME INFO

Core Idea
Move across Chernarus, manage water, food and stamina —
and survive long enough to matter.

What is this?
ArmA 2: Operation Arrowhead mod inspired by the original DayZ Mod experience.
Explore Chernarus, where movement, water and food matter.
No zones. No objectives. Just the world — and other players.

First Minutes
• Spawn near the coast
• Reach land and recover stamina
• Use roads (reduced stamina drain)
• Find water (pumps, wells, fountains)
• Find food (pumpkin fields)

Movement
• Unarmed = fastest and most efficient
• Armed = slower, drains more stamina
• Roads are easier than terrain

You can outrun players — not bullets.

Water & Food
• Stand still ~60 seconds to consume
• Water must be below 80 to drink
• Sources can run dry

Use carefully — the world is shared

Loot
• Found in towns near water sources
• Look for green crates
• Empty = looted or spawned elsewhere

Far north/west requires preparation

Containers
• Water canister (10 water)
• Food bag (20 food)

• Fill while using sources
• Drop (unarmed + crouch ~20 seconds) to use

HUD
You normally see only Water, Food and Stamina.

To see detailed HUD:
• Unarmed → crouch ~20 seconds
• Armed → open inventory (G)

Interaction
• Unarmed + crouched
• Actions take time — commit

Action Speed
• Fastest → unarmed
• Medium → pistol
• Slowest → rifle lowered (2× CTRL)

Stamina
Drains faster when:
• running
• armed
• off-road

Move smart to survive longer

Saving
• Sit (') ~180 seconds
• Save via action menu

Important:
• Only position is saved
• Items do NOT persist
• Hide them in the world

Night
Server skips 21:00 → 04:00

To sync:
• Sit ~180s → save → relog

Stash
• Airfields have storage crates
• Persist through server restarts
