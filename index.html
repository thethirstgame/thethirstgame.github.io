🧪 The Thirst

Survival game built on ArmA 2

Focused on realistic dehydration, scarcity, and world-based persistence.
This is not a loot hoarding game — survival depends on planning and movement.

⚙️ Core Systems

💧 Thirst (Water)

🍖 Hunger (Food)

⚡ Stamina

Drain depends on your actions:
• Running / crawling → high drain
• Walking → moderate
• Sitting ( ' key ) → lowest

🚰 Water & Food

• Drink from pumps, wells, and fountains
• Water is finite but regenerates over time
• Players can empty water sources deliberately
• Food available from pumpkin fields

🎯 Loot

• Random loot spawns across the map
• Weapons include: Makarov, M9, M1911, AK, M16
• Loot locations are hinted
• Maps often found near bus stops

💾 Persistence (Important)

• You CANNOT log out with items
• All items stay in the world
• You must physically store your gear

Safe storage locations:
• North West Airfield
• Balota Airstrip

💾 Save System

To save your position:

1. Use "SAVE POSITION" (mouse scroll)
2. Stay still for 20 seconds
3. Log out after saving

If you don’t — your data may be lost.

🔁 Server

• No regular restarts
• Persistent world
• Restart only with warning
